The Reference tab contains all the placeholders at your disposal. These placeholders can be entered anywhere in (a) the email's Subject Line field and (b) the email content fields. They will be replaced with the corresponding value.

The placeholders are arranged in three sections:

Global Placeholders Global placeholders can be used in any template and any form.
