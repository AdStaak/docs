{% include screenshot.html item="i305.gif" %} If you no longer need a form, you can completely remove it from Form Tools by going to the Manage Forms page and clicking its DELETE link. Please be very careful! Deleting a form cannot be undone - all data will be deleted.

As of version 1.3.0 you will be redirected to a separate page to explicitly delete a form. This will let you know which files (if any) that had been uploaded through that form's fields and are slated for deletion.
