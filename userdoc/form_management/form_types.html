{% include screenshot.html item='i208.jpg' %} There are two types of forms in Form Tools: Internal and External. When you click on the "Add Form" link in the menu, it will direct you to a page where you need to choose one of the following types. Here's what each type means:

  • Internal forms are created within Form Tools and are only found there - not elsewhere on your site. Only Form Tools user accounts will have access to the form. You create and configure the form directly through the interface.
  • External forms are forms that already exist on your website, or somewhere on the web. Up until Form Tools 2.1.0, all forms were external.
