Creating the Settings

{% include screenshot.html item="i358.gif" %} Creating the settings is very simple. Just select the module from your Modules page and click the "Add Setting" page. There, define whatever information you want to have stored. You can choose between a number of different field types, to simply the process of the data entry.

When you've added your field, go to your Settings » Main tab to see how it looks.
