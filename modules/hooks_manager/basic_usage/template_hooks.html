Template Hooks

Template hooks let you add your own HTML into Form Tools pages, letting you do things like add additional fields to forms, extra display text, or adding in extra markup or javascript. With a little imagination, you can use template hooks to expand the built-in appearance and functionality of Form Tools in virtually any way you want.
