Examples

The following pages provide a few examples of how this module can be used.

Adding an email template placeholder
This example explains how to create a custom email template placeholder, in this example a banner image that you can use throughout your HTML email templates.
Client account expiry dates
This example works requires the Extended Client Fields module; it lets you add a simple expiry date option to your client accounts.
Tracking "last emailed date" for submissions
This example explains how to create a "last emailed date" field for your form submissions which logs the last time you or your clients sent an email to an email address associated with a form submission.
