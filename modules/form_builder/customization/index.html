Customization

You don't need any technical knowledge to use the Form Builder: you can create and publish forms on your website just by using the Form Tools and Form Builder interfaces. But for those of you who want to learn a bit more, and maybe develop some custom designs for your forms, you may want some more technical information - and that's the purpose of this section. It explains the ins and outs of Template Sets in fine detail, from the overall design to the individual Template Types themselves.
