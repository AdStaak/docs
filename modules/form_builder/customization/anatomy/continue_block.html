Continue Block

The Continue Block is used to display the continue button at the bottom of your form and Review pages, to allow the users to proceed through the form. All forms have at least two pages: a form page and a thankyou page, but it may contain up to 8 pages: 6 form pages, a Review page and a Thankyou page.

The only requirement for this template is that it contains an HTML submit button with a name attribute of "form_tools_continue". The button can have whatever text you want on it, and be styled however you want.
