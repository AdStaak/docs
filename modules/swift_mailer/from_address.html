From Address Overridden

One thing to beware. Depending on your SMTP service, you may find the "From" email address being overridden by whatever email address is specified in the module settings. Apparently, this is sometimes done automatically to prevent sending emails from accounts that they weren't really sent from, as part of a "phishing" attack.
