Labels / Text tab

{% include screenshot.html item="i242.gif" %}

The Labels / Text tab lets you control many of the more important labels and blocks of text on your form. Here's an explanation of each field and section.

1. Facebook Form

This section contains those settings relating to how your form looks on Facebook itself.

Form Title By default, this setting is set to the name of your form. But you are welcome to customize it here.
Opening Text Anything you enter here will appear just below the form title on your form.
Submit Button Label This controls the text on the submit button.

2. Offline Form

As discussed in the Taking your form offline section, this section lets you customize the content of the page when the form is offline.

Offline form page title The title of the "Offline" page.
Offline form page content The content of the "Offline" page.

3. Thankyou Page

When a user submits the form, they're redirected to a simple "thank you!" page. This section lets you control the contents of that page.

Thankyou page title The title of the "Offline" page.
Thankyou page content The content of the "Offline" page.
