Keeping track of who logs in

{% include screenshot.html item="i91.jpg" %}

One final feature that the module offers the option to log what user logs in and when.

The functionality is very basic. In the Submission Accounts module pages, select the form you're interested in and click on the Users tab, as shown in the screenshot. That lists all users with the date at which they last logged in. Clicking on the View link will take you to the main administrator page to edit the submission.
